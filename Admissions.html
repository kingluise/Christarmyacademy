Admissions

We welcome prospective students and their families to Christ Army Academy, where we provide academic excellence rooted in Christian values. Our admissions process is straightforward and supportive.

Admission Process

  1. Visit the school or call to make inquiries.
  2. Obtain and complete the application form.
  3. Submit required documents (birth certificate, passport photo, last school result).
  4. Attend an entrance assessment and interview.
  5. Receive admission decision and payment details.

Requirements

  • Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Last school report card (if applicable)
  • Medical report (for new students)
