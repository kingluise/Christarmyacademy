About Christ Army Academy

Christ Army Academy is committed to providing a nurturing and spiritually enriching environment where students excel academically and grow in character. Founded with the vision to cultivate future leaders grounded in Christian values, we offer comprehensive primary and secondary education.

Our mission is to equip students not only with knowledge but also with wisdom, integrity, and a passion for lifelong learning. We strive to inspire each child to realize their full potential through holistic teaching, spiritual guidance, and community engagement.

At Christ Army Academy, we pride ourselves on a supportive and inclusive community. Our dedicated staff work closely with families to ensure that every student enjoys a safe, stimulating, and caring learning environment.
