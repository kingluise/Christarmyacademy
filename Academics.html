Academic Programs

At Christ Army Academy, we provide a holistic education tailored to meet the needs of learners at different developmental stages. Our curriculum combines academic excellence with Christian character building.

Nursery Section

Our nursery classes lay the foundation for learning through fun, interactive, and nurturing experiences that develop curiosity and social skills.

Primary Section

In the primary school, we focus on numeracy, literacy, and Christian values to help pupils grow into confident and well-rounded individuals.

Secondary Section

Our secondary school prepares students for national and international exams, offering strong academic support and spiritual guidance.

Extra-Curricular Activities

We believe in the development of the whole child. Our school offers programs in music, arts, sports, leadership, and clubs such as Debate, Drama, STEM, and Young Entrepreneurs.
